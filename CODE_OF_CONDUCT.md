# 🤝 Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to providing an open, welcoming, and secure engineering environment for contributors and maintainers alike, free from harassment or bias, regardless of background, identity, or tenure.

## Our Standards

- **Positive Actions**: Using welcoming and inclusive language, offering constructive peer reviews, and respecting different engineering viewpoints.
- **Unacceptable Actions**: Trolling, personal attacks, publishing private information without consent, or behavior that compromises the physical or digital safety of our community.

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported to the platform safety team at `compliance@nexuscore.network`. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.
